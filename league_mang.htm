LEAGUE MANAGER

  

 

Sport Systems Online objective is to help manage and organize sport leagues more efficiently by minimizing the manual process that this entails. For that purpose, we offer the following functions:

 

1. Different levels of accessibility that will be controlled by the league’s commissioner

2. Create and manage multiple leagues, divisions, and teams.

3. Summary Reports that will help the commissioner manage the league

4. Messaging center that will allow the league’s commissioner to communicate with participants, volunteers, and parents at the touch of a button

5. Calendar of events with automated reminders of games and important dates
